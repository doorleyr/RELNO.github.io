Engineer | Data Scientist



I specialise in developing advanced statistical models which help us to tackle the biggest urban challenges of the 21st century. I'm currently working with the City Science group at the MIT Media Lab where I create predictive models of individual choices leading to complex urban dynamics including traffic, residential mobility and use of amenities. I also create intuitive user interfaces to allow non-experts to interact with and understand the implications of these models. My work applies spatial analysis, machine learning and optimisation with the goal of enabling evidence-based urban planning and design of mobility systems. I have a PhD in transportation engineering from Trinity College, Dublin. My thesis focussed on modelling of transportation networks and quantifying and optimising the impacts of sustainable transportation interventions.
